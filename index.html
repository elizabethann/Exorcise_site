What is Exorcise?
Exorcise is a 2D ghost-themed sidescroller where you recieve game resources based on how much you've exercised in the real world. This idea is loosely based on the freemium game model. But unlike freemium, you progress in Exorcise by creating better health habits, not by killing your wallet.
These game resources are mainly supplied by the number of miles you've traversed. This creates a direct correspondense between your activity in the real world and your rewards in the game.
Miles
How does it work?
The FitBit is a widely used activity tracker which records stats from how many steps you've taken to how many calories you've burned. We pull our data for your game character directly from your FitBit account. All it takes is a simple log-in authorization and you can directly link your game progress with, well, your progress!
